## Post my score
Let a user superimpose their score over a photo (from their gallery or taken
in-app) and share it to social media.
- Score overlay options: full round score sheet (with total + classification),
  just the final score + classification, or "beat my PB by X" + classification
  and position.
- User positions the overlay on the photo before posting.
- Post to a selected social account (from the ones they've added) or "post to
  all".
- Always append a link to logginhood.com to the bottom of the post, with the
  main logo as a clickable link.
- Allow adding special effects / fun emojis to the overlay.
